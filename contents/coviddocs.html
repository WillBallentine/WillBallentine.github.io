Covid-19 Dashboard

A dashboard to analyze the impact of Covid on US States.

Covid-19 has had a severe impact on our world in the last two years. I wanted to have a way to quickly analyze and understand that impact in a more granular way. So I built this dashboard that utilizes a dataset from the CDC which contains the daily reports from each US state over the last almost 3 years. The way this dashboard is setup is meant to allow you to compare a state's total number of cases to its total number of deaths or compare cases or deaths between two states to see the variance in the impact.
